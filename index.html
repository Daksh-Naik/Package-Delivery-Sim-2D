Can you give me a brief over-view?


First of all, this game is 2D (Top Cam Display).

Basically, you have to deliver 5 packages, to 5 distinctive houses (that look different from the background houses).
You will be told where the houses are by keeeping an eye to the dashboard on your Right.

The dashboard will show your Distance travelled (actually, it is in pixels, but represented as meters), time driven and time driven in the opposite lane (counted by pressing the UP ARROW key) , packages left and packages yet to be delivered
and the houses yet to deliver the packages.

You will be given 40 Seconds initially, you will be given more seconds everytime you deliver a package.


  • You have to dodge : Road-works and the Traffic.
  • Each time you deliver a package, the count increases.
  • You cannot go out of the canvas (seriously, why do you want to go out of the canvas?).

  • You are given feedbacks by : Distance travelled, total time survived, Driven Time and the amount of packages delivered / left, and time left.


Game Controls / Keybinds


Press UP ARROW to move forward (up).

Press DOWN ARROW to move backward (down).

Press LEFT ARROW and RIGHT ARROW keys to steer / rotate.


Press W key to move forward (up) faster.

Press D key to drop a package at a house.

Press F5 or E key to restart / refresh the game.

Tips and Tricks

There are some tips that you can implement to win :

  • I recommend you keeping a hand around or on W and D keys and a hand on Arrow keys. There's no use of your mouse in this game.
  • Drive upon the solid line (center line) more, rather than staying within the lanes. By this your chances of not hitting the traffic will be reduced.
  • When dropping the packages, stay away from the traffic and go up to the house door to drop the package.
  • I recommend you not to drive faster (i.e. don't press the W key), instead, drive using the arrow key. There'll be enough time for you to deliver the packages.
  • Press both UP ARROW keys and DOWN ARROW keys to drive even slower, in case if you have to tail-gate the vehicle infront of you.
  • Enable Browser Hardware Acceleration (If you have a GPU) to play the game in good frame rates.


Known Bugs and Problems

There are some bugs in the game which you may have noticed :

  • Traffic Vehicles teleport right infront of you when near a house : While this issue is quite rare, a fix will be deployed soon.
  • Over-lapping Road-works : This has to do something with frame-count properties and will be fixed soon. However, higher-FPS gameplay won't be having these problems.
  • Disappearing Traffic and Road-works : It's the life-time property that destroys the traffic after a certain period of time. This is done to fix lag issues.

  • Insignificant FPS Drops : It has to do something with your browser. I recommend you to close all background programs and browser-tabs before playing this game. This game is all dependant on CPU Usage and GPU (if you've enabled hardware acceleration).
  • Or, Enable Hardware Acceleration to transfer text and graphics rendering all towards to your GPU
  • Can't press 3 arrow-keys at once : This is all dependant on your Keypad / Keyboard. Lenovo and DELL laptops have these problems.
  • Game Canvas too big : If you're using a Device that has lesser resolution (eg. lesser than 1360x630), this is because the initial game-canvas has been set to this resolution, so kindly switch over to a higher resolution device to play or switch to full screen without being distracted by the scroll.
  • Stuck at black screen : This means that your browser is fetching the files and images. If it takes too long, check your internet connection, or reload the page, or check your Disk / CPU usage.
  • Stuck at white screen after black screen : You are using a device that has lesser resolution than the game canvas. Switch to a higher screen resolution to play. This is done in order to fix position properties in the game.
